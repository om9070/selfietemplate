Photo ID Verification Process

• Click the Selfie Button

Once prompted, click on the selfie button to start the verification process.

• Capture Your Photo

Ensure that your face is well-lit and visible, Please also make sure you are holding either your Valid driver license or your SSN. Capture a clear photo while keeping a neutral expression.

• Review Your Photo

Take a moment to review your selfie. Make sure it is clear and matches the photo on your ID.

•Submit Your Photo

Ensure that your face is well-lit and visible, Please also make sure you are holding either your Valid driver license or your SSN. Capture a clear photo while keeping a neutral expression.
